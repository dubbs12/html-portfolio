The 3 Best Movies of All Time

(According to Christopher. Okay, maybe not the "best" but really good, imho)

Nefarious

A creepy and startling movie about demonic possession. Considered by Fr. Carlos Martins (a real life exorcist) to be the best movie Hollywood has ever done on the subject.


Prey

An excellent installment in the "Predator" franchise. Must watch it with the Comanche dub and your preferred language subtitles to get the best experience.


Gladiator

Who doesn't like watching the evil emperor get his comeuppance at the hands of a wronged general turned gladiator?
